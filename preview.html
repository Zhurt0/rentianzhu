Biography

I'm a PhD Student in Economics at the Copenhagen Business School, specializing in Labor Economics and Innovation. I hold an MSc in Advanced Economics and Finance from Copenhagen Business School and a Bachelor's degree in Global Supply Chain and Logistics Management from The Chinese University of Hong Kong.

My research experience includes working as a research assistant on the project Digitalization: Jobs, Firms, and Households at Copenhagen Business School, where I extensively worked with Danish register data and patent data.

I have also had the privilege of working at the Data Science Lab at Statistics Denmark, where I worked on hierarchical text classification models with consumption data.
